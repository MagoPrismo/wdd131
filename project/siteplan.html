Coelho's Restaurant

This name represents the surname of the family that runs the restaurant focused on traditional foods.

Overview

Purpose

The site will be used to present the restaurant, and also to order and make a reservation. This will be used to give some suggestions about what to order too.

Scenarios

* What is the special offer and recomendation for today?

* How can I contact Coelho's and make my order?

* Where is their contact information?

Color Schema

#e8e8ee: color for the body background and light words with dark bg.

#000000: color for the header and footer background, and for some details.

#07002e8f: color to highlight some parts with a dark background.

Typography

Rowdies for heading and titles, such as in h1

Roboto for subtitles, such as in h2 and h3

Open-sans for pagraphs and simple texts, such as in p or label
